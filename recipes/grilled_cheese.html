Grilled Cheese

Description

Two slices of perfectly cripsy and buttery bread encase the wonderful gooey goodness that is the exquisite melted cheese which stretches for miles.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of your bread of choice
  • 2 slices of your cheese of choice
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Heat pan
  2. Butter bread
  3. Put bread in pan
  4. Wait, and then put cheese
  5. Melt that stuff
  6. Eat
